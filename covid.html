COVID



Symptoms
Do you have fever?
Do you have cough and fatigue?
Do you have loss of taste or smell?
Do you have sore throat and nausea?
Do you feel muscle or body aches?
Do you feel difficulty in breathing? 
        
If you have any three or more of the above
 mentioned symptoms you may have this disease.

Treatment is aimed at relieving symptoms and includes :

  • Rest
  • Fluids
  • Use hand sanitizer
  • Pain relievers

Mild medicines

  • ORS
  • Vitamin C
  • Ibuprofen
