Influenza



Symptoms
Do you have fever and chills?
Do you have Headache?
Do you feel fatigue (tiredness)?
Do you have vomiting and diarhea(though this is more common in children than adults)?
Do you have muscle or body aches?
Do you have runny or stuffy nose?
Do you have cough and sore throat? 
        
If you have any three or more of the above
 mentioned symptoms you may have this disease.

Home Remedy :

  • Drink plenty of liquids: choose water,juice and warm soups to prevent dehydration.
  • Take rest: get more sleep to help your immune system fight infection.
  • Consider pain relievers.

Mild medicines

  • Oseltamivir(tamiflu)
  • Zanamivir(Relenza)
  • peramivir(Rapivab)
  • Baloxavir(Xofluza)
