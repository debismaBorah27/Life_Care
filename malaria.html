Malaria



Symptoms
Do you have fever and chills?
Do you have Headache?
Do you have abdominal pain and diarrhea?
Do you feel vomiting and nausea?
Do you have muscle or joint pain?
Do you have rapid breathing and rapid heart rate?
Do you have cough? 
        
If you have any three or more of the above
 mentioned symptoms you may have this disease.
