Pneumonia



Symptoms
Do you have chest pain when you breathe or cough?
Do you have fatigue?
Do you have fever,sweating and shaking chills?
Do you feel vomiting and nausea or diarrhea?
Do you have cough with phlegm? 
        
If you have any three or more of the above
 mentioned symptoms you may have this disease.

Home Remedy :

  • Water, Tea and soup: You need lots of fluids to stay hydrated.
  • Use a warm compress: It's a soothing way to cool your body down from the outside.
  • Soothing steam: Take warm baths or showers so you can breath in the steam.
  • Try turmeric: It helps to treat breathing problems,pain and fatugue.
  • Stay away from smoke: Cigarette smoke can make your symptoms worse.
  • Exercise for your lungs: Breathing exercises can give your lungs a boost as you get over pneumonia.

Mild medicines

  • Azithromycin
  • Clarithromycin
  • Tetracycline
  • Amoxicillin
