TYPHOID



Symptoms
Do you have fever and chills?
Do you have Headache?
Do you have abdominal pain with diarrhea?
Do you feel vomiting,nausea and weakness ?
Do you have loss of appetite?
Do you have skin rashes? 
        
If you have any three or more of the above
 mentioned symptoms you may have this disease.

Home Remedy :

  • Increase Fluid Intake:Typhoid may cause vomiting and Diarhoea that might lead to severe dehydration.
  • Use cold compresses:To combat high fever ,use cold compresses to bring down the temperature.
  • Have Apple Cider Vinegar:Apple Cider Vinegar helps to maintain a proper PH in body.
  • Have Garlic:The antimicrobial properties present in garlic help fight off typhoid bacteria.
  • Have Bananas : Bananas can bring down the fever and treat diarrhoea in people with typhoid.
  • Have Oranges: Oranges are loaded with large amounts of vital vitamins ,minerals and nutrients

Mild medicines

  • Antibiotic: Cephalosporins
  • Antibiotic: Macrolides
